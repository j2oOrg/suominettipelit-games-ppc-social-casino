Privacy Policy

By using Suominettipelit, you acknowledge this Policy.

  1. Data we collect

    We collect basic usage data, device and browser details, and any information you submit through forms.

    This includes the name or email you share for the newsletter or contact form.

    We do not collect payment details because the site does not sell or process purchases.

  2. Cookies

    We use essential cookies for preferences and optional cookies to understand how the site is used.

    Your consent choice is stored locally so we do not ask you every visit.

  3. How we use data

    We use data to run the site, respond to messages, and improve performance and accessibility.

    We do not use data for targeted advertising.

  4. Sharing

    We do not sell personal data. We share only with service providers who help us operate the site.

    Providers are limited to basic hosting and email services.

  5. Retention

    We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes described here.

    If you ask us to delete data, we will remove what we can unless we must keep it for legal reasons.

  6. Security

    We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is perfectly secure.

    Please keep your device secure and use a trusted network.

  7. Your rights

    You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data, subject to applicable law.

    We may ask you to verify your identity before responding.

  8. Contact

    Questions? Email hello@suominettipelit.example or use the contact page.

    Tell us what you need and include the email you used to contact us.

  9. Changes

    We may update this Policy from time to time, and the effective date will change.

    We will post a new effective date when changes go live.
